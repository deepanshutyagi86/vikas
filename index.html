    The Origin

    It Started With a Mother's Love.

    In a warm, bustling kitchen filled with the scent of toasted grains. A brother stood quietly, watching his sister bake biscuits—not for business, not for profit, but for her children. Every ingredient she used had a purpose. Whole wheat for nourishment, jaggery for natural sweetness, and not a trace of chemicals or preservatives.

    That moment stayed with him.

    He began to wonder—if a mother can go this far to ensure her children eat something real and healthy, why isn't the same care reflected in the biscuits we all consume every day?

    That question became a mission.

    And that's how Muchhad Biscuits was born.

    The Spark

    "Why should only babies get the good stuff?"

    — Vikash Bhaiya

    Vikas realized the biscuits were not only healthy but incredibly tasty. The spark was lit—why not share this wholesome goodness with everyone?

    Two Minds,
    One Batch.

    Vikas knew he had stumbled onto something special in his sister's kitchen. But turning a homemade, health-first recipe into a biscuit for everyone required more than just passion—it required scale without losing the soul.

    That's when he teamed up with his senior, Vipul Arya. With Vipul's operational mind and Vikas's uncompromising vision for quality, they set out to build a bakery that felt like a home kitchen.
